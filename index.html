Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney+ ?

+

Disney+ is the destination for the worlds of Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star, and more.

What can I watch on Disney+?

+

Disney+ offers you a continuously growing collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and movies
  • Timeless classics from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Entertaining stories including comedies, dramas, real-life inspired tales, and more
  • National Geographic documentaries, special productions, and much more.

What is the price of a Disney+ subscription?

+

You can choose from three Disney+ subscription tiers:

  • Premium (€11.99/month or €119.99/year): Resolution up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR. 4 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos sound on compatible content.
  • Standard (€8.99/month or €89.99/year): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content.
  • Basic (€5.99/month): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous streams. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content. Includes ads.

Which devices and platforms can I use with Disney+?

+

Disney+ is available on mobile devices, web browsers, game consoles, set-top boxes, and smart TVs.

Does a Disney+ subscription come with a commitment period?

+

Disney+ has no commitment period, and you can cancel your subscription anytime. The cancellation will take effect
at the end of the paid period.

  1. Sign in at www.DisneyPlus.com
  2. Select your profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select your subscription
  5. Select Cancel Subscription
  6. Select Continue to Cancel
